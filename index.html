About Us

ASDI was established in 2010 and formerly known as Design 3D-AE. The company provide services for architectural and structural design, quantity take off and cad-drafting. Due to rapid growth of technology and innovation the company adopted the process of BIM and change the company name to Architectural and Structural Design Innovations (ASDI) in 2015.

ASDI offers a quality of service in design and consultancy. It also offers training in Computer Aided Design and Computer Aided Engineering software to College Students, Professionals and Non-Professionals in developing their skills and equip them in advance technology in Construction, Architectural and Engineering Industry.

ASDI is equipped with high specifications of computers and offers a low-cost training fee included with training materials. Instructors are experts and experienced professionals.

Modelling a Project

  • Understanding scale and unit
  • Adding project levels and grid
Basic Building Elements
  • Adding and modifying columns
  • Adding and modifying framing
  • Adding and modifying floors
  • Adding and modifying exterior and interior walls
  • Adding and modifying doors
  • Adding and modifying windows
  • Adding ceiling
Adding Stairs and Railings
  • Straight stairs
  • Winder stairs
  • U-shape stairs
  • Custom stairs
  • Placing and modifying railings
Other Design Elements
  • Placing rooms
  • Area color scheme
  • Adding furniture
Annotation, Tagging, and Dimensioning
  • Text annotation
  • Placing and tagging rooms
  • Tag doors, windows, and walls
Refining the Building Model
  • Wall components
  • Curtain wall
  • Curtain wall doors
  • Sweep walls and reveal
Documenting a project
  • Duplicating and modifying views
  • Section and elevation views
  • Call out views
  • Creating title blocks
  • Placing views
